DOG

Dogs and humans have the same type of slow wave sleep (SWS) and rapid eye movement (REM) and during this REM stage dogs can dream.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 10 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: Forests

The domestic dog is a member of genus Canis (canines) that forms part of the wolf-like canids, and is the most widely abundant carnivore.
